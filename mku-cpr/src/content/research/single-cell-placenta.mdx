## What we are doing

Existing placenta atlases are built almost entirely from European and North
American samples. We are constructing the first single-cell and spatial
transcriptomic reference of the Kenyan placenta — healthy, malaria-exposed,
and exposed to environmental pollutants — in partnership with the Max
Delbrück Center.

## Why it matters

Reference data shapes every downstream tool. Atlases that exclude African
populations bake that exclusion into every cell-type classifier and
diagnostic that follows.
