## What we are doing

The Centre's three-year delivery target is at least one diagnostic candidate
in field validation. This theme runs the translation pipeline — from a
discovery-stage biomarker to a usability-tested device with a regulatory
pathway in Kenya and a route to WHO prequalification.

## Why it matters

A discovery that does not reach an antenatal clinic in Bungoma is, for the
women in Bungoma, indistinguishable from no discovery at all.
