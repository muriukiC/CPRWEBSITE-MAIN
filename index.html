Transforming Maternal & Newborn
Health Through Research

Our work on placental malaria tackles one of the most critical challenges in maternal and newborn health. Using advanced histopathological analysis and biomarker discovery, we decipher the unique molecular mechanisms of placental infection and develop innovative point-of-care diagnostics for resource-limited settings.
