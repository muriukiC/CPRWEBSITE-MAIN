## What we are doing

Kenya introduced a rubella-containing vaccine into its national schedule in
2016. We are tracking the consequences — for maternal seroprevalence, for
incident congenital rubella syndrome, and for the residual pockets of
susceptibility that remain.

## Why it matters

Vaccine policy without surveillance is policy without feedback.
