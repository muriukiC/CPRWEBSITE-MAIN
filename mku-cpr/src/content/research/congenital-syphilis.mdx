## What we are doing

Congenital syphilis remains the leading preventable cause of stillbirth in
several Kenyan counties. We are developing a field-deployable LAMP assay for
*Treponema pallidum* that runs from a finger-prick at the antenatal clinic,
returning a result before the woman leaves.

## Why it matters

Every undiagnosed maternal infection is a child lost or born harmed.
Diagnostics that work in a 14-bed rural clinic, on a hot day, without
refrigeration, are a different problem from diagnostics that work in a
reference laboratory.
